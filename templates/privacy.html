Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your email address when you register and the expense data you log (amounts, categories, and dates). We also collect basic usage data to improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking services you signed up for. We use it to generate your spending reports and manage your account. We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

We do not share your data with third-party marketing services. We may use limited third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) that are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and do not have access to your raw financial data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at privacy@spendly.example.com.

Your privacy is our priority.
